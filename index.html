Who We Are

ICW Ghana is a youth-led, impact-driven initiative dedicated to improving community health, education, and social well-being across Ghana. As part of the global ICW network, we work with students, professionals, and partners to design sustainable projects that respond to real community needs.

Our Mission

To reduce the impact of cancer in Ghana through education, early detection, community outreach, and patient support.
